A Bad Website

Below is a link to what I believe is a bad website

adamandeveddb.com.

This website is bad because:

1. There are a whole bunch of vibrant images where each one commands attention, causing a constant shift in focus.

2. Cells are so small that the front page looks dense and heavy.

3. Not all of the cells are working.

4. There is nothing under the contact folder, making it useless.

5. The images take up to much space in the websites.

6. Little gifs play in the main page that are distracting.

7. It takes a while for the links and folders to respond.

8.It takes a while for the page to load.

9. The overall color scheme is pretty boring and generic.

10. The overall placement of images, text and gifs is not consistent between different subpages.
